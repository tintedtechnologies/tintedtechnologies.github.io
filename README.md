# Tinted Technologies

Official website for **Tinted Technologies LLC** - Cloud & AI architecture for teams building what's next.

## About

We help individuals, small businesses, and teams build real skills and make smart technology decisions.

### Services

- **Tech Foundations & Career Enablement** - Train individuals to break into tech: cloud basics, AI literacy, real-world tooling, and career-ready skills.
- **Small Business Cloud & AI Advisory** - Practical consulting to help small businesses get online, modernize operations, and use cloud + AI responsibly.
- **Applied AI & Automation** - Identify where AI actually helps. Process automation, internal tools, and efficiency, without overengineering.
- **Workshops & Enablement** - Hands-on learning or internal upskilling on modern cloud + AI.

## Tech Stack

- **React** + **Vite** for fast builds and development
- **React Router** for client-side routing
- **CSS Modules** for scoped styling
- **Markdown** blog posts with frontmatter
- Deployed on **GitHub Pages**
